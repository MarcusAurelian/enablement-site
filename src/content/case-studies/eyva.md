## The state we found

Eyva had an SDR sending ~160 emails per week manually, a 14% open rate, and a founder spending most of his nights re-writing campaigns that weren't landing. *"We don't have a people problem,"* he told us on the first call. *"We have a system problem."*

## What we built

Full domain rebuild — six sending domains, warmed for three weeks, DKIM/DMARC/SPF re-aligned. A new sequence architecture built around three intent signals: site-visit, role-change, and competitor switch. The SDR moved from list-building to call execution, gaining ~20 hours per week.

## The result

Open rate climbed from 14% to 47% by week 8. Meetings per week doubled from 2 to 4. The founder pulled out of the daily campaign work entirely.

## What's next

Eyva runs in our "We Run It" track. We operate the engine; their SDR runs the calls. Weekly review, monthly metrics deep-dive.
